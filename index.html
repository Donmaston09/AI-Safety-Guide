Community Safety Lab

Use these Africa-relevant scenarios to spark discussion in schools, public workshops, youth programmes, and health education settings.

🔑
Strong passwords
Use long, unique passwords for every account. A password manager makes this effortless — one master password secures everything else.
Learn more →
📱
Turn on MFA
Multi-factor authentication adds a second check. Even if a criminal steals your password, they still cannot access your account.
Learn more →
🔄
Keep software updated
Updates patch security holes. Enable automatic updates so you're never exposed by a known vulnerability criminals can exploit.
Learn more →
🎣
Watch for phishing
AI now helps criminals write convincing fake messages with perfect spelling. Pause before clicking links — verify senders independently.
Learn more →

Facilitator / Trainer Mode

Ready-made sessions for organisations, schools, and community workshops.

Session 1 — Introduction to AI Safety (30 min)

Covers what AI is, why it matters for personal safety, and the concept of digital footprint. Suitable for beginners with no technical background.

Beginner 30 min Any audience

Session 2 — Recognising AI-Powered Threats (45 min)

Deep dive into deepfakes, voice cloning, phishing, and social engineering. Includes live demos and a practical spotting exercise.

Intermediate 45 min Adults / workplace

Session 3 — Core 4 Behaviours Workshop (60 min)

Hands-on workshop where participants set up a password manager, enable MFA, and practise identifying phishing attempts in group exercises.

Hands-on 60 min Bring devices
